Before using:
* All new operators need to be trained before they can mill on their own
* Check that the fire extinguisher is present and that you know what to do in case of fire

Rules for safe use:
* Everybody around the machine shall wear safey goggles and hearing protection before the machine is powered up
* Do not leave the machine unattended
* Verify that you have a undamaged and sharp milling bit and use correct settings

Efter using:
* Never leave broken fasteners in the spoilboard
